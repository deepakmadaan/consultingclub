 BITS Pilani Consulting Club 


BITS Pilani Consulting Club


BITS Pilani Consulting Club is a student association in pursuit of excellence in management consulting which seeks to equip the student body with resources to make informed decisions on consulting careers, tools necessary to build successful careers in consulting and opportunities to promote extra-curricular excellence.
The club provides avenues of interaction among students, professionals, faculty and alumni through organized activities that advance management consulting outside the classroom environment.



Mission Statement


The objectives of the Consulting Club are:

  • To equip the students with the tools and resources necessary for careers in the consulting domain.
  • Case interview module: Initiating a module to train students on how to approach case interviews
  • Corporate Presence on Campus: These initiatives are aimed at creating more corporate presence of consulting firms on campus and thus creating more learning,internships and networking opportunities for the students.
  • The club encourages participation in consulting events and case competitions , conducted by premier B-Schools and management associations like CIMA.
