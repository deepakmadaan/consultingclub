About Us

BITS Pilani Consulting Club is a student association in pursuit of excellence in management consulting which aspires to endow the student body with resources and tools to make successful consulting careers and opportunities to promote extra-curricular excellence. We believe we have the vibrant technical expertise amalgamated with strong management skills to bring a fresh perspective to the consulting environment.

Mission

Our goal is to create opportunities for career exploration and development in consulting domain for the students through programs including case practice sessions, case competition, and pro bono consulting and educational workshops. Our vision is to create a knowledge sharing ecosystem of the BITSians present in the consulting field around the globe and students on campus.
